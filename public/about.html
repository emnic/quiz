About

An attempt to exercise the brain before the afterwork, as we will do the opposite there, and to just have some fun before we all leave for Christmas!

Game Idea

This is a level based game. If you manage to find the correct answer you will be taken to the next level. And hopeylly when all levels are done you're one of the top three.

Rules

There are no rules, everything is allowed.

Prices

If you're one of the top three lucky winners you will be presented a secret password. Be sure to remeber this password, because you will need it to collect the price during the afterwork.

Tips and Tricks

Keep an open mind and seek for a soultion where you least expect it. And a call for help, might be a good idea if you have been stuck for a while.
